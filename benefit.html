What is the Medmetry Nurse?
Medmetry’s nurses fill temporary assignments at the nation’s top facilities. Their experience and knowledge in a range of disciplines and specialties allow them to jump in and quickly adapt to their facility’s needs, no matter what new vicinity they find themselves in.
Our Recruitment Team Has Your Back..
Each of our nurses is supported by a Medmetry recruitment expert that has only one thing in mind: to get you the best assignment possible that aligns with your personal and professional goals.

As nurses’ advocates, they work tirelessly to expedite the recruitment process. They’re the ones that stay on top of all licensing and verification requirements, and can provide you with career advice and guidance so that your career is always headed in the right direction.
Let Your Nursing Career Fit Your Needs.
Nurses already have a lot on their plates. That’s why Medmetry has streamlined everything from screening to onboarding to ensure that you can find your next best role that’s 100% aligned with your needs. With us, your demands will always be put on the forefront so that you can perform your position to the best of your abilities.
